The Mountain Room

The Mountain Room invites you to experience the majesty of the mountains with a cozy, cabin-like vibe. Featuring soft textiles, deep colors, and mountain-inspired decor, this room is ideal for anyone seeking a peaceful, nature-infused getaway.

Occupancy and Rates

Suitable for two guests, with rates starting at $297 per night, including a hearty homemade breakfast and an optional free, private hike up a mountain trail of your choice.

Inspired by the nearby peaks, The Mountain Room combines rugged charm with cozy comforts. The room features a king-sized bed with a dark wood frame and thick, woolen blankets to keep you warm on cooler nights. Real-wood walls, nature photography, and vintage mountain maps create an atmosphere that feels both adventurous and tranquil. A small seating area near the window offers the perfect spot to enjoy the view or unwind with a book after a day of exploring.

Amenities

  • King-sized bed with luxurious linens and warm wool quilts.
  • Private balcony with a glorious mountain view.
  • En-suite bathroom with a rainfall shower and pine-scented bath products.
  • Mini coffee station with fresh ground coffee and a French press.
  • Selection of hiking guides and local adventure books.
  • Rock polisher—perfect for discovering beauty from the local stones through creating keepsakes from your stay, polished to a smooth, lasting shine.

Located on the upper floor, The Mountain Room's private balcony offers sweeping views of the mountains and forest, allowing you to feel fully immersed in the landscape.

For adventurers and daydreamers alike, The Mountain Room is a haven that mirrors the grandeur of the outdoors while providing a cozy, restful space to recharge.
